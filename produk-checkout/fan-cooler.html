The CPU Super Freeze

Gaming is hot, literally! If you're burning up noobs on your mobile game then it's only a matter of time before your phone starts burning back (queue the sweat).

This is where the Fun Cooler has your back. Inspired by an actual household refrigerator the Fun Cooler combines a freezer chip and turbo fan, to ice down temperatures by 14°C in just one minute.

High speed low sound

The FunCooker uses a Thermal Electronic Cooling system, heat is absorbed from the surface of your phone using the Peltier effect and released out towards the fan system.

Seven fan blades boasting a spinning speed of 6200 RPM make quick work of the heat released from the Thermal Electronic Cooling system. Wondering how fast 6200 RPM is? That's twice the speed of a helicopter rotor.

The fan may move faster than a helicopter but it sounds
